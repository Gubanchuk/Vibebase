  content generation, with caveat that the effect depended on the LLM
  getting the level right (<5% overshoot).

# Concrete takeaway

The AI tutor shouldn't just "speak B1" — it should calibrate per turn using a
recent mistake signal. A drift of even ~5% above level significantly
attenuated the acquisition effect.
