# Narrow reading principle

Instead of jumping between topics (one chapter about sports, next about
cooking), **stay on one topic for 3-5 texts**:

- Day 1: article about climate change policy
- Day 2: longer article, same topic, overlapping vocab
- Day 3: opinion piece, same framing
- Day 4: scientific abstract, deeper
- Day 5: novella chapter with climate theme

By day 3, 60%+ of topic-vocab repeated → learner processes faster,
freed attention для новых words + complex structures.

# Why it works (cognitive view)

- **Reduced cognitive load**: topic-vocab automatised
- **Schema activation**: brain knows frame, predicts next sentence
- **i+1 наверняка achievable**: each text только marginally harder
- **Depth > breadth**: understand ONE topic deeply vs 5 topics shallowly

# Practical for AI-tutor

- **Lesson sequencing**: Teacher/Curator group лессоны по topic-family
  (all tenses together, all business vocab together)
- **Reading recommendations**: v2 feature — AI suggests 3-article
  narrow-read sequence on topic пользователь готов нырнуть
- **Not jumping modes**: Session 1 — native chat, Session 2 — lesson on
  same topic, Session 3 — flashcards same topic. Cross-mode narrow focus.

# Применимо у нас

В Curator'е: prerequisites-graph должен encourage sequential mastery.
Если user mastered «Present Perfect basics», next auto-suggest —
«Present Perfect continuous», не jumping в Conditionals. Narrow path
через related topics.
