# Concrete prompt rule

In Mode 1 (Native), instruct: "Every turn, aim for 95-98% of content to be
vocabulary and structures the learner already knows. Mark any word you
introduce that's above their level with a gloss like `word — meaning` in
parentheses. Never use more than 2 above-level items per turn."
