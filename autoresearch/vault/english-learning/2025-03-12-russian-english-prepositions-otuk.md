# Flashcard seed

These are exactly the pairs Mode 4 (Flashcards) should pre-populate for a
Russian speaker at B1:

- depend on (not "of")
- marry someone (not "with")
- listen to (not "listen")
- in my opinion (not "on")
- rely on (not "at")
- discuss something (not "about")
- attend a meeting (not "on")
- share something with someone (not "to")
