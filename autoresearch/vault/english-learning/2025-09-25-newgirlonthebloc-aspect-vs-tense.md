# Curriculum sequence for skill "Learning English → Tenses"

1. Present Simple vs. Continuous (aspect-first)
2. Past Simple
3. Past Continuous
4. Future Simple + "going to"
5. Present Perfect (biggest jump — introduces "now-connection")
6. Past Perfect
7. Perfect Continuous forms (last — polish, not foundation)

Skipping ahead to Perfect before aspect is internalized is the #1 dropout
predictor per the article.
