# Короткие descriptors

### A1 (Breakthrough)
Can understand and use familiar everyday expressions. Introduce self.
Answer basic questions about self.

### A2 (Waystage)
Can understand sentences about topics of immediate relevance (shopping,
family). Describe past experiences in simple terms.

### B1 (Threshold) — **critical milestone**
Can deal with most travel situations. Produce simple connected text on
familiar topics. Describe experiences, events, dreams briefly with
reasons.

### B2 (Vantage)
Can understand main ideas of complex texts, including technical
discussions in their field. Interact with fluency and spontaneity with
native speakers without strain for either party.

### C1 (Effective Operational Proficiency)
Can express ideas fluently and spontaneously without much obvious
searching. Use language flexibly for social, academic, professional.

### C2 (Mastery)
Can understand virtually everything. Summarize from different sources
coherently. Express spontaneously very fluently and precisely.

# B1 как threshold

B1 — это уровень где learner может **travel independently**. До B1
английский — курс. С B1 — инструмент. Большинство взрослых learners
нацелены на B1-B2 как достаточный ceiling для work + travel.

# Practical implication

Placement-тест должен reliable распознавать B1 границу. Наш 15-question
static bank делает это примерно (score 60% → B1). Для точности — можно
усилить question-bank 5 дополнительными B1-discriminator-questions
(конкретно Present Perfect, articles, indirect speech).

# Применимо у нас

CEFR scale — authority для English Curator'а. Топики учебного плана
маркируются `levelMin` / `levelMax` из CEFR. Teacher адаптирует lexicon
под уровень. Progress в UI — «B1 ⟶ B1+ ⟶ B2» — очень readable.
