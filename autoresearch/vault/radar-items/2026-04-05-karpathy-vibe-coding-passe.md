# Implication for the radar

Our "vibecoding" radar should broaden its scope to include agentic-engineering
patterns. "Vibecoding" as a tag stays (it's our product vocabulary), but
content coverage should emphasize orchestration, review, and oversight
techniques — not just "AI wrote my code".
